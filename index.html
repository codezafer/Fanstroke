About Us

FanStroke is a decentralized fantasy gaming platform built on NEAR Protocol, which offers players the ability to join and compete in fantasy sports leagues. Whether you’re a casual player or a hardcore fantasy sports enthusiast, FanStroke has something for everyone. The platform lets anyone participate in various leagues such as Cricket, Football, NBA Rugby, etc, and win prizes.
